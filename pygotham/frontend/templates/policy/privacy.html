Privacy Policy:

Information we collect:
We collect user names, email addresses and passwords. Passwords are encrypted, and we have no way of retrieving them. if your password is lost, you must reset it through our forgot password link.

We securely prompt for credit card information and pass it to Braintree, our payment processor. We do not store this anywhere in our system, and it is purged from system memory after use.

What we do with this information:
We take privacy very seriously. We use email addresses to send updates and conference related info to attendees. It is not used for any other purpose, nor is it given or sold to any other party, including other attendees or sponsoors. After the conference ends, we destroy this data.

Feel free to contact nycpythonorganizers@googlegroups.com for details involving privacy.
